What is biohumus and what is its composition?

{% endblock header_content %} {% block content %}

Vermicompost is a product of processing organic masses by California red earthworms. It is a loose, finely granulated mass with granule sizes of 1-3 mm, possibly up to 5mm. Concentrated fertilizer contains in a balanced combination a whole complex of nutrients - micro and macroelements, mineral fertilizers, amino acids, humic acid, humates and fulvates of sodium and potassium, natural phytohormones, spores useful for plants of soil microorganisms, gibberellins in an easily digestible form.
Благодаря интенсивной ферментации усиливается:

  1. Plant growth and development.
  2. Increases germination and seed germination energy.
  3. Stimulates root formation in plants.
  4. Increases the immunity of plants.
  5. Increases fertility, looseness of the soil.
  6. Keeps moisture in the soil for a long time.
  7. Reduces the content of nitrites in agricultural products.
  8. Prevents the entry of heavy metals and radionuclides into the plant.
  9. Increases the content of sugars, proteins and vitamins in fruits and vegetables.
  10. Eliminates chlorosis, stimulates flowering and fruiting.
  11. Gives an effect to the rapid ripening of fruits for 7-10 days.
  12. Stress is relieved, especially when planting plants, seeds.
  13. The products grown on vermicompost practically increase the duration of storage.
  14. Vermicompost is 10 times higher than manure in terms of nutrient content. Vermicompost is 10 times more effective than manure. And fresh manure it is a focus of fungal and viral diseases of plants. The vermicompost completely lacks viable weed seeds, has antiseptic properties, destroying viral and fungal diseases of plants.
Chemical composition of Vermicompost
Appearance Homogeneous crumbly mass from dark brown to black. The presence of inclusions of CRE and their cocoons is allowed.
Mass fraction of organic substances, %, not less 55%
Mass fraction of moisture, % 40-45%
Mass fraction of ash, % 35-40%
Organic substances 55-65%
Mass fraction of Humus, % 28-45%
pH of 1% suspension 6.8-7.2%
Friability, % 100%
Total nitrogen N, % 1.5-4.8%
Total phosphorus (P2O5) %, not less 2.1-3.5%
Total potassium (K2O) %, not less 2.3-3.4%
Mass fraction of dry residue, %, not less 45%
Calcium 4.0-6.0%
Magnesium 0.6-2.3%
Iron 0.6-2.5%
Manganese 60-80 мг/кг
Mass fraction of heavy metals, mg/kg below the MPC for soils
Pathogenic microflora absent
Helminth eggs absent
