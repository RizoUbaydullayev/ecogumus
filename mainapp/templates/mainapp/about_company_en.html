ABOUT THE COMPANY
Ekogumus was officially founded in 1997, but the first steps towards its development and prosperity were made back in 1988 by our father, the founder of the company - Haitboy Solizhonov. We continue to expand and develop our father's business. For more than thirty years, the company has become a leader in the agricultural industry, providing effective solutions for agriculture around the world.

The company became known as "Ekogumus" and began its journey with a small plot of land for breeding California worms and obtaining high-quality fertilizer - Biohumus. Subsequently, the laboratory of the Fergana State University began to be used for analysis at the beginning.

Despite limited resources, the team of scientists worked with inexhaustible enthusiasm and determination. The first years were not easy, but thanks to scientific talent and hard work, dedication, they were able to develop a unique type of organomineral fertilizer.

Every year Ekogumus expanded its research and production capabilities. Over time, their fertilizers have become in great demand among farmers and agriculture around the world. The company actively collaborated with agronomists to adapt its products to different soils and climatic conditions.

The success of Ekogumus continued to grow, and it became not only a leading producer of organic fertilizers, but also a leader in agricultural research. The company invested in the development of new technologies, which allowed them to create even more efficient and environmentally friendly products.

Over time, Ekogumus has become known as a symbol of innovation and quality in agriculture. She has been actively involved in sustainable rural development, providing knowledge and resources to increase yields and improve the lives of local farmers.

Today, more than thirty years after its foundation, Ekogumus continues to delight its customers with innovative products and solutions that contribute to the growth of agriculture and the well-being of society. Her efforts and dedication to agriculture have become a real contribution to ensuring food security and prosperity of world agriculture.
