Ekogumus was officially founded in 1997, but the first steps towards its development and prosperity were made back in 1988 by our father, the founder of the company - Haitboy Solizhonov. We continue to expand and develop our father's business. For more than thirty years, the company has become a leader in the agricultural industry, providing effective solutions for agriculture around the world.
We are the only holder of the certificate of conformity for the serial production of organomineral fertilizers in the country

Solizhonov Haitboy - Candidate of Biological Sciences, associate professor, professor, worked at FERGU. He had a cherished dream to revive the soil of his native land. Back in 1988, together with a group of enterprising scientists and agricultural experts, he decided to join forces to create an innovative company for the production of organic fertilizers with the help of California red earthworms. The founder grouped theory with practice. They saw a huge potential in the development of a unique fertilizer that could significantly increase the yield and quality of crops.

The company became known as "Ekogumus" and began its journey with a small plot of land for breeding California worms and obtaining high-quality fertilizer - Biohumus. Subsequently, the laboratory of the Fergana State University began to be used for analysis at the beginning. Despite limited resources, the team of scientists worked with inexhaustible enthusiasm and determination. The first years were not easy, but thanks to scientific talent and hard work, dedication, they were able to develop a unique type of organomineral fertilizer.
At the moment, we have more than 1,000 students (followers) in Uzbekistan, Russia and other Central Asian countries who adhere to the technological regulations and production instructions for obtaining high-quality organic mineral fertilizer Biohumus.
