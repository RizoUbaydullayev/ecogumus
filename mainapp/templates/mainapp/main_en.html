Ecogumus was founded in 1993 with the aim of producing innovative mineral fertilizers. For twenty years, the company has become a leader in the industry, providing effective solutions for agriculture around the world.
The leading producer of organic mineral fertilizers in Uzbekistan

Back in 1993, a group of enterprising scientists and agricultural experts decided to join forces to create an innovative company for the production of mineral fertilizers. They saw a huge potential in the development of unique fertilizer formulas that could significantly increase the yield and quality of crops.

The company became known as "Ecogumus" and began its journey with a small laboratory in a small town. Despite limited resources, the team of scientists worked with inexhaustible enthusiasm and determination. The first years were not easy, but thanks to scientific talent and hard work, they were able to develop several unique formulas of mineral fertilizers.
